ABOUT US

Founded in 2017 and based in Abeokuta, Nigeria. Grandeur Tailors is a custom tailor shop with a strong commitment and dedication to excellence...





We believe there is nothing better than a tailor-made outfit, and our mission is to give that to everyone who wants it. We've been crafting native wears for Nigerian men for over 10 years.





OUR IDEAL CLIENTS ARE MEN WHO REALLY CARE ABOUT WEARING QUALITY

We cater for the native wears that makes everyone feel confident, by choosing designs and styles that fits them. We strive to meet the personal need of every client that does business with us.






AFFORDABLE AND DURABLE HIGH QUALITY PRODUCTS

We deliver high quality products that are affordable yet made of durable materials.With our custom-made clothing, our client will be able to find their desired fabrics, styles, and colours. We personally hand-stitch every article of clothing and focus on providing a high level of customer service which leads to customer satisfaction...






VARIETIES OF STYLES THAT BOOSTS YOUR CONFIDENCE

We have a good number of style options available making it easy for clients to pick their choices or specify any modifications they want. We are eager to answer any questions you may have over the phone or via Whatsapp. We look forward to working with you to provide high quality men's clothing that will deliver self-confidence and help you look your very best
